Kim Soon-duk

This page is dedicated to Kim Soon-duk, who has dedicated her life to demanding redress and official apology from Japan for the military sexual violence that affected over 200,000 women during World War II.

About

Kim Soon-duk was born in 1928 and passed away in 2004.
